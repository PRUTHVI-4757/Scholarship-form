About Us

  • Academic Performance: Many scholarships require a minimum percentage or grade in previous examinations.
  • Income Limit: Some scholarships target students from economically weaker sections with specific income thresholds.
  • Category or Community: Some scholarships are reserved for SC/ST/OBC, minorities, or differently-abled students.
  • Course or Level of Study: Scholarships may be available for specific courses (e.g., engineering, medicine) or study levels (e.g., undergraduate, postgraduate).
  • Institution Type: Some scholarships apply only to students studying in government-recognized institutions.
  • Geographical Location: Some scholarships are targeted at students from specific regions or states.
  • One-Time Registration (OTR): Students need to register once to apply for scholarships. An active mobile number and Aadhaar are required.
  • Application Process: Students can apply for scholarships and track their application status through the portal.
  • Eligibility and Deadlines: The portal provides detailed information about eligibility criteria, required documents, and deadlines for each scholarship.
  • Check Eligibility: Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria, including academic performance, income limits, category, and course of study.
  • Register on the Portal: If using a platform like the National Scholarship Portal, create an account with personal details, an active mobile number, email ID, and Aadhaar.
  • Fill Out the Application: Log in and complete the form with accurate personal, academic, and financial details.
  • Upload Documents: Required documents include:
    1. Passport-sized photograph
    2. Mark sheets of the qualifying examination
    3. Income certificate
    4. Caste certificate (if applicable)
    5. Bank passbook (for account details)
    6. Aadhaar or other identity proof
  • Verify and Submit: Carefully review your application before submission. Some platforms allow a preview.
  • Renewal (If Needed): Recurring scholarships may require annual renewal with updated academic and financial details.
